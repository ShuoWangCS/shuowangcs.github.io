Hello, I’m Shuo Wang! I am currently a Associate Research Fellow, School of Data Science, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), China. His reseach interests mainly include machine learning and multimedia data analysis, such as large-scale multimedia indexing and retrieval, multimedia data embedding, and video understanding. He serves as a technical program committee (TPC) member for ACM MM, CVPR, ECCV, IJCAI, etc, and reviewer for TIP, TMM, TOMM, etc.
